ABOUT US

Recognized as Roaster of the Year in 2020—a national once-in-a-lifetime award given by Roast Magazine—Coffee Roasters Co. is proud to have put premiere coffee on the map in San Diego.

COFFEES

HIGH QUALITY

The relationships we have with our importing and producing partners allow us to feature great coffees year after year - whether that’s coffee from a new farm, a new varietal or a special lot from an existing relationship.

NEW COFFEES EVERY MONTH

Every day, we discover exciting coffees from all over the world, so we rotate our coffees every four weeks to give you something new!

FRESH ROASTED

Coffees are roasted every Sunday and Wednesday in Brooklyn, and our roasting team builds on the quality of the beans and the hard work of the farmers, highlighting the nuances and sweetness in each coffee.
